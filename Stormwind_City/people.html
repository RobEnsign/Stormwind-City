People and Culture

National color is blue with the Lion being a common symbol of the kingdom's strength. The demonym used for the people of Stormwind is “Stormwindian”. Most people become peasants; people who craft, sow fields, work mines and/or harvest lumber. Humans make up the largest race of the city's population. Stormwindians revere the “Light” and the Church of the Holy Light plays a significant role in their society with the Cathedral of Light acting as their primary hub of worship. Warlocks are considered as pariahs and are forced to operate in the shadows of polite society. Most Void Elves that reveal their connection to the void are often treated in the same manner. The political structure of the city is that of a kingdom with the King being the head of its government, a position entitled to the male born son of the Wrynn dynasty.
