About

Founded 1,200 years before the events of the First War, located in the northwestern region along the coast of Elwynn Forest on the planet of Azeroth lays the largest human city of Stormwind City, the capital city of the Kingdom of Stormwind. Regarded as being the “heart of the Alliance” the city stands out in their willingness to work with other races and to find diplomatic solutions rather than rely on armed conflict. The site of the city is resting on what used to be the original city after being completely razed during the conflicts of the First War by invading Orcs.
