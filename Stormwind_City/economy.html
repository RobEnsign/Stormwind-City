Economy

The capitals economy is heavily relied on by its agriculture sector, the Trade and Dwarven district while the Harbor is relied on for logistics and trade across the Great Sea to the Kalimdor continent. Even though the capital is the heart of the Alliance, it is perpetually under financial strain because of constant calamities such as the razing by the orcs of the First War, first invasion of the Burning Legion, Deathwing, 2nd invasion of the Burning Legion. And this does not include additional resources that was needed to fight offs the Lich King and his army of undead in Northrend and more local threats such as Defias Brotherhood and the Scarlet Crusade. Much of the taxes collected are from the businesses that reside within the capital and a modicum amount from its citizens. Though it has been suggested that the tax revenue from both businesses and citizens be increased to assist with the perpetual rebuilding of the city and its military. Another source comes from venturing adventurers that partake in the auction house where goods are sold between them, and of course the auction house takes its usual cut of the sales.
